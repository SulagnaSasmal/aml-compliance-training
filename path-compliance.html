Role Notes for Compliance Officers
  • You are responsible for authorizing all SAR filings — no SAR should be filed without your review and sign-off.
  • You have sole authority to clear potential OFAC matches — front-line staff must escalate screening hits to you. Document every disposition decision.
  • You are the institution's primary BSA E-Filing Supervisory User — manage staff filing access and ensure timely submission.
  • Annual recertification is required. This program satisfies the annual training requirement under 31 CFR 1020.210.
  • If you receive a law enforcement request or grand jury subpoena related to a SAR, do not disclose the SAR filing to the subject or their counsel. Consult legal before responding.
