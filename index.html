Regulatory Framework

This training covers obligations under:

BSA / USA PATRIOT Act

Bank Secrecy Act — FinCEN oversight, recordkeeping, CTR / SAR requirements

OFAC / SDN List

U.S. Treasury sanctions programs, Specially Designated Nationals List, blocking requirements

FFIEC / FinCEN Guidance

Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council examination guidance, FinCEN advisories

⚠ Non-Compliance Consequences

Failure to complete mandatory training by the deadline is a policy violation that may result in restricted system access. Individual BSA/AML non-compliance can result in civil money penalties up to $250,000 per violation under 31 U.S.C. § 5321.
